About Tianji

Tianji, Secrets of Destiny is designed to make BaZi more approachable for modern users. It brings structured chart analysis into a refined, readable, English first experience for people who want clarity without unnecessary complexity.

Why Tianji Was Created

Made for Real Curiosity

Many people are curious about BaZi, but existing tools can feel too technical, too culturally closed off, or too dependent on consultant access. Tianji was created to offer a calmer starting point for ordinary users who want private self exploration.

Structured, Not Random

Tianji is built around structured chart logic and deterministic readings. The goal is to give users a more stable and consistent experience than loosely generated fortune style apps.

What the App Includes Today

Launch Features

  • Main pillars and hidden stems
  • Five Elements analysis
  • Traditional analysis
  • ShenSha
  • Advanced analysis
  • Ten Gods
  • Luck pillars

Design Philosophy

Tianji is meant to feel premium, readable, and welcoming. It is not built only for seasoned practitioners. It is designed for people who want a professional experience, plain English guidance, and space to explore at their own pace.

BaZi is the beginning of the Tianji journey. The wider platform is designed with long term growth in mind.

Begin with BaZi

Tianji starts with structured BaZi chart creation and layered analysis, presented through a modern, calm, and readable interface designed to serve a broader audience.
