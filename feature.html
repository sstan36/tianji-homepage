Past Life Insight

Uncover the stories that shape your soul.

Past Life Insight offers a deeper look into your karmic themes, life patterns, and soul lessons, woven into rich, consultation-style narratives designed to enlighten and empower.

Each reading can be exported as a beautiful, shareable PDF that you can save, print, and revisit anytime.

What your reading reveals

Your chart is read in five layers. Each one answers a different question about who you are, what you carry, and what may be waking up in your life now.

1

Summary

Start with your core nature and key traits. A clear snapshot before you open the deeper layers of your soul story.

2

Karmic Foundation

See what you are made of at the deepest level: your soul element, unfinished karmic debt, and the purpose your chart keeps pointing you toward.

3

Past Life Influences

Explore previous life archetypes, patterns that carry forward, soul lessons, and relationship themes that feel familiar for a reason you cannot quite name.

4

Past Life Indicators

Hidden karmic stars, your soul journey stage, spiritual gifts, and timing that hints when certain themes may surface, resolve, or awaken.

5

Life Cycle Karma

Understand where you are on the longer arc: growth, testing, maturity, or renewal, and what your soul may still be here to complete.
